Our Values

We encourage our community to approach their yoga with tons of compassion, self love and humour. We want you to feel amazing in you body and to learn to connect with parts of your body that feel stuck or shut down.

We encourage you to see our studio as a place to heal. Through your poses nourish your body and soul that you may embody a full life.

We encourage you to listen to your body and do what you feel is appropriate and safe.

Our studio is a safe space for you on your mat, where you can move, breathe, fall over, laugh, experience and grow!
