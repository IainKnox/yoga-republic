Yoga & Meditation

Restore balance in the heart of Glasgow city

Welcome to your neighbourhood yoga studio.

An inclusive and diverse Glasgow city centre yoga studio with an emphasis on harmony for body & mind.

The ultimate goal of our yoga studio is to help you achieve the state of peace (Samadhi) and abide in that state.

Our down-to-earth studio is based in the hustle and bustle of the city centre. We want you to feel good in your body, so come join us for live classes including flow, yin, pilates, workshops, meditation, community and more.
